Je kunt niet altijd afscheid blijven nemen

Book typography and cover design for Lisa Weeda's book "Je kunt niet altijd afscheid blijven nemen" ("You can't always take one's leave").

The book is a telling of Lisa Weeda's search for the story behind her Ukrainian family history, using many letters, family pictures, and interviewing familymemers, in praticular her grandmother.

Find the ungoing stream of texts by Lisa on her Tumblr page (mostly in Dutch)
